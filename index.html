Horse riding FAQs

Do I need to wait for other people to form a group?

No, we do not form groups of strangers. Either it’s just you alone, or a group of friends, or a family — come and enjoy the ride with our guides.

Do I need to book in advance?

Yes. At least 1 day before your trip. But the earlier the better.

Are your horses calm?

No need to worry. Our horses are completely calm. It took us years of training and tons of love to provide you with horses who are relaxed and perfect for riders of any level.

Can I trot or canter during the trip?

Our tours are individual. The gait is chosen according to your experience. You can walk, trot, canter or gallop. The most important thing is that you have fun (and it's safe).

I’ve never ridden a horse before. Will this be a problem?

Not at all. We have a riding school as well, and our horses are used to working with beginners.

Is it safe for small children?

Not only it's safe, it's also highly enjoyable for little kids. A guide will walk next to the horse and hold it, so the child does not control the horse himself.

Do you provide safety equipment?

Yes, we have a range of helmets for all ages.

What should I wear?

If the weather is hot we recommend a light long-sleeved top, long trousers (preferably stretchy) and comfortable shoes that fully cover your feet. Don't forget sunscreen. When it's a bit chilly, add some extra layers.

What are your opening hours?

We are open every day and at any time (within reasonable limits).

What if I am scared of horses, but I really want to try?

You are just at the right place. We will help you to overcome your fear and start building your special relationship with these amazing animals.
